Chicken Curry

Description

Just not any ordinary chicken curry but Gurkhali inspired chicken curry originated from the mountains of Nepal which you MUST TRY!

Ingridients

  • Chicken Thighs
  • Onions
  • Tomato
  • Tumeric
  • Ginger & Garlic Paste
  • Lemon or Lime
  • Corriander

Steps

  1. Step 1:Heat up oil until it smokes
  2. Step 2: Deep Fry the chicken and the onions
  3. Step 3: Add spices and ginger & garlic paste
  4. Step 4: Add tomatoes
  5. Final Step: Let it boil and touch it up with corriander
